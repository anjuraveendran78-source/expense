# User Manual - Expense Tracker

## 1. Getting Started

### Registration
1.  Navigate to the **Register** page from the home screen.
2.  Enter your **Username**, **Email**, **Phone Number**, and **Password**.
3.  Click **Register**. You will be redirected to the Login page.

### Logging In
1.  Enter your registered **Username** and **Password**.
2.  Click **Login**.
3.  You will be directed to your **Personal Dashboard**.

---

## 2. Managing Finances

### Adding an Expense
1.  Click **"Add Expense"** in the sidebar menu.
2.  Fill in the details:
    *   **Date**: When the transaction occurred.
    *   **Amount**: The cost of the item.
    *   **Category**: Select (e.g., Food, Travel, Bills).
    *   **Description**: A short note (e.g., "Lunch with colleagues").
    *   **Type**: Choose `Expense` (spending) or `Income` (earnings).
3.  Click **Submit**.

### Viewing Transactions
*   Go to **"Transactions"** to see a list of all your past entries.
*   You can **Edit** or **Delete** entries if you made a mistake.

### Dashboards
*   **Main Dashboard:** Shows a quick overview of your total Income, Expenses, and Balance.
*   **Charts:** Visual graphs help you understand your spending patterns over time.

---

## 3. Family Management

### Registering Family Members
**Only the Head of Family (Admin) can register family members.**
1.  Go to **"Register Family"** in the sidebar.
2.  Enter the member's details (Username, Email, Password).
3.  Share these credentials with your family member so they can log in.

### Family Expenses
*   Use the **"Family Expenses"** page to add costs that are shared by the household (e.g., Rent, Groceries).
*   These are tracked separately from your personal expenses.

---

## 4. Reports & Analysis

### Generating Reports
1.  Navigate to **"Reports"**.
2.  **Filter Options:**
    *   **This Month:** View activity for the current month.
    *   **Last 6 Months:** See trends over half a year.
    *   **Custom Range:** Select specific Start and End dates.

### Export to PDF
*   Click the **"Download API"** or **"Export PDF"** button on the Reports page to save a detailed statement to your computer.

---

## 5. Settings & Profile

### Reset Password (Work in progress)
1.  If you forget your password, click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login screen.
2.  Enter your registered **Email Address**.
3.  Check your email for a **One-Time Password (OTP)**.
4.  Enter the OTP to verify your identity and set a new password.

### Dark Mode
*   Toggle the **Moon/Sun icon** in the top-right header to switch between Light and Dark themes for comfortable viewing.
